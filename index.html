More land, to save more precious lives

We started building this sanctuary from scratch. We had nothing, but through great sacrifices, driven by conviction and love for the mission of saving lives, we managed to acquire a small piece of land where today we care for and protect over 80 rescued animals.

Over the years, we've learned a lot. We've overcome obstacles and found creative ways to provide a good life for more and more rescued animals, always putting the mission of saving lives first. However, the small size of our current land is a significant limitation, and we've reached a point where we can no longer accommodate more animals.

Today, our love for the sanctuary's mission leads us to launch this campaign and ask for your support to continue our work. With your help, the land we are going to purchase will provide us with the space we so desperately need to keep rescuing, caring for, and protecting animals for many more years to come. With your assistance, Santuario Igualdad will continue to be a beacon of hope for animals who are suffering today.

We are counting on you ❤️
Marisol & Ariel, Santuario Igualdad Founders

Fundación Santuario Igualdad

Santuario Igualdad is located in El Monte, on the outskirts of Santiago, Chile. It's a very special place because there, a couple of humans set out to build a safe space to protect the lives of cows, goats, sheep, pigs, and birds who have survived the livestock industry, abuse, abandonment, and cruelty.
